Who We Are ?

At Sylvi, we are a team of passionate individuals driven by a shared vision and mission. We are dedicated to creating and delivering premium wristwatches that embody the perfect fusion of style, quality, and craftsmanship.

Vision

We aspire to establish SYLVI as an market leader in the watch industry, recognized for delivering exceptional value for money and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our mission is to foster a sense of patriotism and pride among every Indian by offering high-quality, made in India watches.
